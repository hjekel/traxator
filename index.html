📖 How to use TraxaTor

1 Select a model

Choose a series and then your specific John Deere model from the dropdown menus.

2 Enter details

Fill in the year of manufacture and the number of operating hours.

3 Select options

Click on any factory options that apply to your tractor. Options affect the estimated value.

4 Calculate

Press the Calculate button to see the estimated trade-in range. You will get a low and high estimate.

5 Compare on the market

Use the market links (Mascus, TractorPool, Ritchie Bros) to compare with real listings.

💡 Tips

The signal badge (GO/CAUTION/STOP) indicates how the hours relate to average usage. Your last 20 valuations are saved automatically and visible in the history section below.

Values are indicative and based on comparable market listings. No rights can be derived from this tool.
